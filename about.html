Our Story

Founded By Editors, Built For Writers

uRevision started in 2014 when a small team of academic editors noticed how often brilliant ideas were let down by unclear writing. What began as a side service for graduate students has grown into a full-service editing platform trusted by novelists, executives and job seekers alike.

  • Subject-matter experts across 40+ fields
  • Every edit reviewed by a second editor
  • Strict confidentiality on every document
